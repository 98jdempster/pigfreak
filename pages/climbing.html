Multi-pitch

  • Banana into Tomato into Gunsight to South Peak - Seneca Rocks, WV

  • June 11, 2025

  • This was my first ever multi-pitch and it was incredible. I've never done any trad climbing so Garrett lead every pitch and I'm so glad he did because the exposure at the top was insane. Looking down while climbing or belaying and realizing you're hundreds of feet from the ground is insane. None of it felt too scary since I was on top rope, but I can't imagine leading it. The first pitch was a fun little 5.6, but it didn't fill me with much confidence when I somehow ended up off-route slightly and had to hang for a second to figure out where I went wrong. The 2nd pitch was also there to humble me. It was only a 5.8, but I somehow managed to use the absolute worst beta possible and make the crux feel insanely muscley and difficult when really there was a much simpler way to do it. Once that nightmare pitch was over we did the last 2 pitches as 1 pitch and that was very easy despite the insane amount of exposure. I absolutely loved doing this, but I fear I may still be a boulder bro at heart.
